✅ Good Daily Habits / चांगल्या सवयी
🌅
Early Rising
Wake by 5:30 AM. Brahma muhurta — 90 mins before sunrise — is the most powerful time for study and meditation.
💧
Water Therapy
Drink 2 glasses of warm water immediately after waking. Flushes kidneys, stimulates digestion, and hydrates cells.
🍽️
Mindful Eating
Eat slowly, chew 32 times per bite. Avoid screens while eating. Enjoy meals at fixed times daily.
📖
Daily Reading
Read for 20 minutes daily — books, not social media. It reduces stress by 68%, builds focus and vocabulary.
🤲
Gratitude Practice
Write 3 things you're grateful for each morning. Rewires the brain to notice positivity and abundance.
😴
Quality Sleep
Sleep by 10 PM. The body's peak repair cycle is 10 PM–2 AM. Keep your room cool, dark and phone-free.
🚿
Cold Water Bath
A cold shower boosts immunity, increases alertness, improves circulation and builds mental discipline.
📵
Screen Detox
No phone for the first 30 mins after waking and last 30 mins before bed. Protect your mind's peak state.
🏃 Exercise / व्यायाम
🚶
Walking
30 min brisk walk daily lowers bad cholesterol, reduces blood sugar, strengthens heart and burns 150 calories.
🏋️
Strength Training
2–3 days/week of bodyweight exercises (squats, push-ups) protects joints, builds metabolism and bone density.
🧗
Stretching
10 minutes of stretching every morning improves flexibility, reduces back pain and injury risk significantly.
🏊
Swimming
Full-body workout with zero joint impact. 30 mins of swimming burns 400+ calories and builds lung capacity.
🚴
Cycling
20 min of cycling strengthens legs, improves heart health, reduces carbon footprint and boosts serotonin.
⏱️
Consistency Wins
20 minutes daily is better than 2 hours on weekends. Build the habit first — intensity follows naturally.
🧘 Yogasanas / योगासने
🧘
Surya Namaskar
सूर्यनमस्कार
12-step full-body sequence that stretches every muscle group, improves circulation, energises the body and calms the mind.
⏱ 5–10 mins / day
🌬️
Pranayama
प्राणायाम
Anulom-Vilom (alternate nostril) and Kapalbhati breathing purify lungs, reduce stress and improve brain oxygen supply.
⏱ 10–15 mins / day
🏔️
Tadasana
ताडासन
Mountain Pose. Improves posture, strengthens thighs, knees and ankles. Relieves back pain from desk work.
⏱ 1–2 mins / day
🦩
Vrikshasana
वृक्षासन
Tree Pose. Builds concentration, balance and coordination. Strengthens spine, legs and pelvic muscles.
⏱ 1 min each side
🐍
Bhujangasana
भुजंगासन
Cobra Pose. Opens the chest, strengthens the spine, stimulates abdominal organs and relieves lower back pain.
⏱ 30 sec × 3 sets
🌙
Shavasana
शवासन
Corpse Pose. Releases tension in every muscle. A 10-minute shavasana equals 30 minutes of deep sleep in rest quality.
⏱ 10 mins after yoga
🦅
Paschimottanasana
पश्चिमोत्तानासन
Seated forward bend. Stretches spine and hamstrings, massages abdominal organs, reduces obesity and menstrual issues.
⏱ 1–3 mins / day
⚡
Vajrasana
वज्रासन
Thunderbolt Pose. Only yoga pose safe after meals. Aids digestion, reduces acidity and strengthens pelvic muscles.
⏱ 5–10 mins after meals
